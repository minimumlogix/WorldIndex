# Cyber Neon

High tech, low life. Mega-corporations and hacker syndicates clash in a neon-drenched metropolis.

## The Great Net Expansion
Following the collapse of traditional governments, multi-trillion dollar corporations took over control of the city grids. The lower sectors, neglected and submerged in toxic rain, became breeding grounds for cyber-enhanced gangs and elite hackers.

* **Megacorp Hegemony**: The corporations enforce total data control.
* **The Glitch Revolution**: Decentralized code factions attempt to liberate the Net.

## The Undergrid
Below the light streams lies the Undergrid, where dark net markets, bio-hacking clinics, and rogue AI agents trade forbidden protocols.
