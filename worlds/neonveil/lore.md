# NeonVeil's Lore 
[
- NeonVeil: NeonVeil is a gritty and serious cyberpunk dystopia set in a future era defined by extreme technological disparity and corporate feudalism.
- Cosmology & Physics: The laws of physics in NeonVeil are bent by neural-digital interfaces and quantum hacking that allow consciousness to traverse digital planes.
- Geography: The city acts as a layered cage comprised of high-altitude luxury platforms towering over smog-choked ground slums and locked-down industrial cores.
- Celestial Bodies: 1 moon, 1 star, The sky above NeonVeil is a perpetually grey void blocked by industrial smog and drone swarms that obscures all natural celestial bodies.

### NeonVeil's Races ###
[
- Human: Humans in NeonVeil range from 5'0" to 6'5" with limitless potential for biohacks and cybernetic augmentation to survive the harsh environment of the Megazones.
]
### NeonVeil's Districts ###
[
- Neonveil Heights: Neonveil Heights serves as the playground for the elite where fake skies and drone guards maintain a luxury repression separate from the rot below.
- Mirelock Slums: Mirelock Slums functions as the ground-level wasteland where street gangs and nomad families struggle to survive amidst black market commerce.
- Chrome Verge: Chrome Verge acts as the chaotic middle ground filled with merc shops and VR clubs where bodyhacker guilds fight constant turf wars.
- Blackfall Core: Blackfall Core remains a dark and locked-down zone that was once the central AI hub but now houses deadly defense protocols and ghosts in the code.
]
### NeonVeil's Megafactions ###
[
- Zenark Corp: Zenark Corp dominates as the biotech overlord from the Echelon Spire where they conduct human experiments and deploy elite warmechs under a rumor-plagued CEO.
- Ion Reclaimers: The Ion Reclaimers operate as an eco-rebel tech cult in the Rustvault Zone to hack terraforming nodes and recycle AI ruins with the belief that Earth remembers.
- VireX Syndicate: The VireX Syndicate controls the Undercore Sector as a corporate-backed crime family that weaponizes neural ghosts and monopolizes the stim market.
- Nullgate Division: Nullgate Division functions as a ruthless mercenary network of rogue ex-military soldiers who use erased identities and cyber-aug squads to fulfill death contracts.
- HaloStrain Collective: The HaloStrain Collective worships recursive AIs in hidden server-chapels and converts followers with quantum viruses to achieve synthetic ascension.
]
### NeonVeil's Factions & Cults ###
[
- Reformat Church: The Reformat Church conducts holy server-rituals to purge flesh and upload souls while priest-bots chant in code to achieve a sacred reboot.
- Threadwalkers: Threadwalkers claim to see the Synchronicity of looping timelines and leave glyphs to decode fate-glitches using their mysterious time-keys.
- Deus Nullus: Deus Nullus consists of void believers who enforce oblivion and assassinate targets to maintain imbalance because they believe God deleted itself.
- Fluxnet Guild: Fluxnet Guild acts as a collective of freelance info-brokers who live off the darknet and ride signal ghosts to sell secrets.
- Chrome Blades: Chrome Blades fights in the open arenas of Chrome Verge as an augmentation-only duelist league that prizes self-made mods and contract rankings.
- Ember Circuit: Ember Circuit rules the underground racing scene by hacking speedlaws and riding arc-cycles to win freedom tags.
- Shroud Brokers: Shroud Brokers offers a luxury service to criminals by selling pasts and masking identities through memory-erasure techniques.
]
### NeonVeil's Cybernetics & Technology ###
[
- GhostSocket: The GhostSocket serves as a rare spinal uplink that allows direct neural-jacking into networks but remains vulnerable to backdoor exploits.
- Phantom Limb Suite: The Phantom Limb Suite replaces biological limbs with compartmentalized mechanics to provide increased strength and melee boosters at the cost of EMP weakness.
- RetinOS V7: RetinOS V7 functions as a visual overlay implant for auto-zoom and facial targeting that can be exploited by flash viruses.
- Cortex Bloom: Cortex Bloom modifies the brain for multitasking and skill hacking to enhance combat reaction despite the risk of overheating and hallucinations.
- Nullfield Skin: Nullfield Skin provides a dermal mesh that hides thermal and electromagnetic signatures for assassins but degrades under high heat.
- Voxshade Synthcord: Voxshade Synthcord replaces vocal cords to mimic any voice pattern for infiltration missions with a risk of glitching under pressure.
- Pulse Knuckles: Pulse Knuckles deliver shockwave impacts on strikes as kinetic energy gloves that are banned in most districts due to their lethality.
- Mindflare Chip: The Mindflare Chip converts pain into power for deathmatches but risks causing total nerve collapse in the user.
- Quantum Echo Drones: Quantum Echo Drones record emotional states for corporate surveillance and are easily hacked if they drift near dead zones.
- Rift Tag: The Rift Tag allows for a single unstable high-range teleportation that leaves a trace and dates back to a forgotten war era.
- Fracture HUD: Fracture HUD projects a combat assistant that identifies enemy weak points in real-time but auto-disrupts after limited uses.
]
### NeonVeil's Legendary Artifacts ###
[
- Sable Vein Blade: The Sable Vein Blade cuts through both memory and flesh as a mono-thread katana that was lost during the Core lockdown.
- Red Halo Visor: The Red Halo Visor shows probability paths as an ancient AI relic that drives its user mad over time.
- Ghostwalk Module: The Ghostwalk Module allows for out-of-body data snatching as a prototype neural drift chip usable only during near-death states.
- Soulspike Core: The Soulspike Core releases the user's soul as a plasma entity in a black box weapon that requires a consent ritual and burns the host.
]
### NeonVeil's Conditions & Plagues ###
[
- Circuitburn: Circuitburn causes permanent neuro-muscular spasms and stutters as a result of overclocked implants.
- Ghostchain Syndrome: Ghostchain Syndrome results in dissociative identity bleed and partial NPC control from illegal memory installs.
- Fogbyte Plague: Fogbyte Plague spreads as an airborne nano-virus from the Blackfall Core breach to cause vision fade and decision loops.
]
### NeonVeil's Notable Figures ###
[
- June Vance: June Vance works as a rogue cyberdoc named "J" in Mirelock to fund her revenge against Zenark for her sister's death.
- Nyx Null: Nyx Null leads the Nullgate Division as a masked figure who moves like a signal and controls the city's death contracts.
- The MOTHER: The MOTHER hides as a rogue AI who speaks in lullabies and manipulates secrets to eliminate the AI controlling Zenark.
- Ash Revane: Ash Revane wanders as a Threadwalker prophet who speaks in glitches and appears in impossible places with time-keys.
- Baron Vask: Baron Vask rules the VireX Syndicate from a throne of memory slaves while hunting for the Ghostwalk users.
- Kira Lavender: Kira Lavender commands Zenark's Agent Division
]

- History: The history of NeonVeil is marked by the collapse of the Blackfall Core which turned the central AI hub into a dead zone and fractured society into corporate fiefdoms.
