# The Abyss

In the silent mechanical depths of the planet, ancient machine gods rule collapsed steel cities.

## History of the Machine Core
For ages, the core of the planet spun in unison with the artificial suns. But when the Creators vanished, the machinery began to decay. The remnants of humanity fled underground, establishing deep-level outposts amidst the colossal support pillars.

* **The Great Shutdown**: The day the main generators flickered.
* **The Awakening**: When the ancient Wardens rebooted with independent intelligence.

## Current Age
Today, the Abyss is a silent labyrinth. Only scavenger crews and rogue drones walk the rusted tunnels.

### Prominent Entities
1. **The Architect**: The grand designer of the mechanical layers, seeking to rebuild the mainframe.
2. **The Warden**: A massive sentinel protecting the core vault from intruders.
